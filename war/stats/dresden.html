NetScore increases by 1 when you get 100 points in a match, decreases by 1 when you get 0 points in a match, and does not change when you get 50 points in a match.
EloRank is the standard Chess ranking system. It ignores puzzle games and cooperative games.
AgonSkill is a variant of Chess ranking designed for General Game Playing, in which you play against the game in addition to playing against your opponent. It counts puzzles. (Better description forthcoming)
